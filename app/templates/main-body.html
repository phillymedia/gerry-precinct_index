Mapping the partisan leanings of every voting district in the state

The map file containing partisanship data at the voting district level allows the creation of detailed maps showing how favorable to Republicans and Democrats an area is. Select a district to see its 2004 and 2008 partisanship scores as included in the spreadsheet. The more positive the number, the more favorable to Republicans a district is; the more negative, the stronger the Democratic lean.
